Environmental assessment certificates and permits issued under the Mines Act and the Environmental Management Act (EMA) are the primary provincial authorizations for major mine projects in British Columbia. The Ministry of Energy and Mines (MEM), Ministry of Environment (MOE), Environmental Assessment Office (EAO) and Ministry of Forests work together to provide integrated oversight of British Columbia’s mining sector.
